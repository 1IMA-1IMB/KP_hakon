HISTORY

EARLY LIFE

The Dark Urge is a Bhaalspawn, but unlike other Bhaalspawn, they weren't born, they were created from the dead flesh of Bhaal himself. The Dark Urge grew up outside the Cult of Bhaal, and were at some point taken in by a couple. They had a relatively peaceful childhood for a time, before succumbing to the urge and murdering their loved ones and foster parents

THE PLAN

Eventually, the Dark Urge became leader of the Cult of Bhaal, vowing to bring about the extinction of all life in existence in honor of their father. During this time, fellow Bhaalspawn Orin the Red joined their ranks and served under the Dark Urge.

Prompted by Bhaal's visions, the Dark Urge collaborated with Enver Gortash to steal the Crown of Karsus from Mephistopheles' Vault. Their goal was to create a soulless mind flayer army for mass annihilation, including their own demise. Despite planning betrayal, the Dark Urge allied with Gortash and Ketheric Thorm, the chosen of Bane and Myrkul.

THE BETRAYAL

At Moonrise Towers, the Chosen raided the mind flayer colony, using the Crown to control the elder brain. During the operation, the Dark Urge formed an unique connection with the elder brain, aligned with their plan. Initially revered as a god, the Dark Urge's status changed when betrayed by their jealous sister, Orin the Red, resulting in a pierced skull and infection, marking them as the first True Soul.

Discovered by Kressa Bonedaughter, the Dark Urge's resilience intrigued her. Kressa experimented on the Dark Urge, rendering them speechless and inclined toward violence. The Elder Brain termed the Dark Urge a "True Soul," inspiring Gortash's plan for more True Souls and the false religion of "the Absolute" for Unity. To end Kressa's obsession, her husband sent the broken Dark Urge on a Nautiloid Ship, waking with no memories.
